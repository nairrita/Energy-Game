Green Energy

Carbon

  Instructions:

   Level 1:

  • You have to generate energy from windmill by passing it through the wind currents

  • The windmill will follow you mouse.

  • You can control the size of the windmill by pressing the "D" key.

  • Generation of wind energy will fetch you points. Try your best to gain as many points as possible

  • After reaching a certain score, you will get to choose it's utilisation

   Level 1:

  • You have to generate energy from the solar panel by passing it through the clouds and placing it in sunlight

  • The solar panel will follow the mouse

  • Generation of solar energy will fetch you points. Try your best to gain as many points as possible

  • After reaching a certain score, you will get to choose it's utilisation
